About Us: Barangay Hingatungan Fisherfolks Association

Story Founded in 1995, our association unites local fisherfolks, fishers, and marine enthusiasts.

We are the heart of Barangay Hingatungan, dedicated to sustainable livelihood, community development, and marine ecosystem preservation.

  • We celebrate our vibrant culture by actively participating in local events and initiatives.
  • Protecting our marine ecosystem is our top priority, with responsible fishing practices and conservation efforts.
  • We support our members through training, financing, and market access projects

Our elected leaders guide us to address community needs and manage resources.Join us to protect our coastal environment and enhance the lives of our fisherfolks. Together, we ensure sustainable coexistence with the ocean, fostering responsible stewardship and shared prosperity.
